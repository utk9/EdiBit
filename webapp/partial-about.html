Edibit was created at NHacks as a web platform where users can buy and sell cheap food. Edibit is catered towards events, conferences, meetings, parties, or anything that sometimes makes more than they can eat. We offer a place where you can sell excess food to earn a little cash, and where people can buy a cheap dinner or lunch.
